Belfast


Belfast is one of the main protagonists in Azur Lane. Her real counterpart is HMS Belfast, now a museum ship. She is a light cruiser for Royal Navy. She can be built in any construction pool. As a playable character, Belfast is the head maid of Royal Navy, always at the orders of the commander and her Majesty, Queen Elizabeth. She is almost obsessed by her maid duty.
