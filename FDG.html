Friedrich der Große


Friedrich der Große is a character in Azur Lane. She is an Ironblood battleship available on Japanese and Chinese servers only, requiring 630 Ironblood research points to be unlocked, and the most expensive ship to develop. There was no actual KMS Friedrich der Grosse, a SMS Friedrich der Grosse did really exist though the Azur Lane character is more inspired by one of the H-class battleship proposals, abandoned when World War II began. She is actually part of the collaboration with World of Warships. As a playable character, when Friedrich der Grosse seems quite impressing by her posture and her big weapons, she is one of the most motherly figures throughout all the game. She takes the commander as her own child, taking care of them. Many of her lines refer her as the leader of a whole orchestra, composed of her weapons, playing a "melody of hatred, fear and despair".
